PAGE 3 — For Investors (investors.html)

Page Headline
Invest With Confidence. Every Startup We Introduce Is Battle-Tested.
What Type of Startups We Bring
We focus on pre-seed and seed stage technology startups in India. Our sweet spot is founders building in software, embedded systems and hardware-adjacent spaces — sectors where our partners have decades of hands-on experience and can genuinely add value beyond capital.
How We Vet Startups
We don't introduce startups to investors cold. We work alongside founding teams for months — stress-testing their financials, sharpening their GTM and strengthening their team. By the time we make an introduction, we've put our own reputation on the line.
What Investors Get
You get curated, battle-tested startups — not raw pitches. Every founding team we introduce has been guided, challenged and strengthened by partners with 100 years of combined experience. Less risk, more confidence.
